How We Help Your Software Engineering Team

Like Pair Programming on steroids! We help groups of individual contributors work together as a unified team in real time, removing delays in typical Software Development Lifecycles.

Building on the principles of agile and test-driven development ensembling enables the delivery of better-quality software sooner. Direct communication and swift decisio nmaking and working on a single item accelerates progress towards the goal.

Upskill, motivate, and engage your team.

Remove waste through Efficiency

Say goodbye to idle engineers waiting for answers, writing lengthy email/chat threads, and being forced to start something new over finishing. Remove key-person risks and siloed knowledge.

Quality Assurance

Catching bugs and technical debt early, preventing maintenance nightmares down the road, code reviews happen continuously, leading to cleaner, more robust solutions backed by repeatable tests.

Focus and Flow

By eliminating context switching and high WIP, teams maintain their flow. Each team member contributes actively, leading to a harmonious rhythm of development as the group only ever works on the most significant user need.

Simplicity

We prioritize delivering usable increments continuously. No unnecessary features or complexity--just elegant solutions that meet user needs with high throughput and lean principles.

High-Quality Solutions For Your Customers

Equip your team with the skills needed for Ensemble Programming. We offer tailored hands-on sessions and practical guidance. We begin with a rudimentary code kata to develop an understanding of the process and roles at play.

We transition smoothly to working on a real-world scenario for your engineering team through Test-Driven Development and hands-on programming. We guide the team through the process, ensuring a seamless shift in your development culture.

We don't stop at implementation. We can revisit and refine practices alongside your team to help remedy issues and reinforce the fostering of a collaborative environment (at a discounted rate).
