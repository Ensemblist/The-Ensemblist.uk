How We Help Your Software Engineering Team

Like Pair Programming on steroids! We help groups of individual contributors work together in real-time to remove waste found in the typical Software Development Lifecycle in order to deliver better quality software sooner, keep staff engaged and motivated, together as a team, All built on the principles of agile and test-driven development.

Remove waste through Efficiency

Say goodbye to idle engineers waiting for answers, writing lengthy email threads. With direct communication, decisions are made swiftly, and progress accelerates.

Quality Assurance

Catching technical debt early, preventing maintenance nightmares down the road. Code reviews happen continuously, leading to cleaner, more robust solutions. Higher quality sooner means happier customers and happier staff with an automated regression pack baked in!

Focus and Flow

By eliminating context switching and thrashing, teams maintain their flow. Each team member contributes actively, leading to a harmonious rhythm of development and the group only ever work on the most important thing.

Lean Simplicity

We prioritize delivering usable increments continously. No unnecessary features or complexity--just elegant solutions that meet user needs backed by a robust set of repeatable tests.
